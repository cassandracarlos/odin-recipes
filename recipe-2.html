Buttered Toast

  1. Take a slice (or more) of bread and place it in the toaster.
  2. Toast the bread until it reaches your preferred level of crispiness.
  3. Once the bread is toasted, remove it from the toaster carefully.
  4. While the toast is still hot, spread a generous amount of butter evenly across one side of the toast using a butter knife.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
